Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from other restaurant-finding apps?

We provide nutrition-focused and personalized information for you. We rank nearby restaurants and dishes by your nutrition goals (e.g., high-protein, low-sodium). You can compare dishes across restaurants in one tap and filter by goals, cuisine, and allergens.

How is this different from other nutrition-data apps?

Most apps log what you ate; we help you decide what to eat by linking nutrition to live restaurant menus. We have a verified badge for restaurants that has passed our inspection to ensure the nutrient data is correct.

How do you obtain nutrition values, and how do you ensure accuracy?

Our AI is trained by human nutritionists to professionally analyse nutrition values of the dishes. Our verified badge system also shows you which information is verified by partner restaurants and human nutritionists.

How do you determine a user's fitness goals?

Onboarding collects basics (age range, height, weight, sex, activity level), dietary preferences and aims (bulk, cut, maintain, medical-safe options), and generate fitness goals according by nutritionist trained AI.
